Chimichurri Chicken Thighs with Jasmine Rice & Blistered Mushrooms

35 minserves 4 quickone-pan protein

Pre-marinated chimichurri thighs seared until crisp, served over fluffy jasmine rice with garlicky pan-blistered baby bellas.

On sale this week

  • Publix Publix Marinated Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs (Chimichurri) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Four Elephants Thai Jasmine Rice Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Baby Bella Mushrooms (sliced, 8 oz) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Bertolli Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Green Avocados On sale, each

From the pantry

  • salt & pepper
  • garlic (or garlic powder)

Steps

  1. Start the jasmine rice: rinse 1.5 cups, simmer with 2.25 cups water and a pinch of salt, covered, ~15 min; rest off heat.
  2. Pat the chicken thighs dry and sear in a hot skillet with a little olive oil, 5–6 min per side until 165°F and caramelized. Rest on a plate.
  3. In the same pan, add a splash more oil and the mushrooms. Cook undisturbed 3–4 min to blister, then toss with a little garlic and salt for another 2 min.
  4. Fluff the rice, slice the chicken, and serve with mushrooms and sliced avocado on top.

The chimichurri marinade is oil-and-herb based — dairy/egg-free. No cheese or crema needed.

Sheet-Pan Salmon with Crispy Smashed Potatoes

45 minserves 4 sheet-panseafood

Roasted salmon fillets with olive-oil smashed russets — one sheet pan, no dairy, big crispy edges.

On sale this week

  • Publix Fresh Salmon Fillets (responsibly sourced) On sale, fresh
  • Publix Publix Idaho Russet Potatoes (5-lb bag) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Bertolli Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix McCormick Grill Mates Seasoning Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • salt & pepper
  • lemon (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil small whole potatoes until fork-tender, ~15 min. Drain and let steam-dry a few minutes.
  2. Smash each potato flat on an oiled sheet pan, brush with olive oil, season, and roast at 450°F for 20 min until crisp.
  3. Push potatoes aside, add the salmon fillets, brush with oil and Grill Mates, and roast 10–12 min more until the fish flakes.
  4. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and serve.

Grill Mates blends are dairy/egg-free, but glance at the label if you swap to a Bacon or creamy variety.

From the pantry

  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • garlic powder
  • salt

Steps

  1. Brown the ground chicken in a skillet, breaking it up, ~6–8 min.
  2. Stir in chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and a splash of water; simmer 2–3 min until saucy.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  4. Fill tortillas with chicken, sliced avocado, and a spoonful of salsa.

Check the tortilla label — most Mission flour tortillas are dairy/egg-free, but a few varieties add milk. Skip the sour cream and queso on sale this week.

Garlic Shrimp & Mushroom Spaghetti

30 minserves 4 seafoodpasta

Olive-oil garlic shrimp tossed with blistered mushrooms and spaghetti — a bright, brothy, no-cream pasta.

On sale this week

  • Publix Publix White Shrimp (large, 31–35 ct, frozen) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Baby Bella Mushrooms (sliced, 8 oz) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Bertolli Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • dry spaghetti
  • garlic
  • red pepper flakes
  • salt
  • lemon
  • parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente; reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Thaw and pat shrimp dry. In a wide pan, cook mushrooms in olive oil until browned, then push aside.
  3. Add more oil, sliced garlic, and red pepper flakes; add shrimp and cook 2–3 min until just pink.
  4. Toss in the pasta with a splash of pasta water and a squeeze of lemon; loosen to a light sauce and finish with parsley.

Standard dry spaghetti is dairy/egg-free; avoid fresh or 'egg' pasta. This sauce is olive-oil based — no butter or cheese.
