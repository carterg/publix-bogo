Sheet-Pan Italian Sausage, Peppers & Potatoes

40 minserves 4 sheet-panone-pan proteinweeknight

Everything roasts on one pan while you do nothing: sweet Italian sausage, tricolor peppers, and russet chunks, all crisped in olive oil.

On sale this week

  • Publix Publix Sweet Italian Sausage $5.69
  • Publix Publix Tricolor Peppers $4.99
  • Publix Publix Idaho Russet Potatoes $3.99
  • Publix Publix Yellow Onions $3.49
  • Publix Pompeian Imported Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • salt & pepper
  • garlic powder
  • dried oregano or Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Cut potatoes into 3/4-inch chunks, peppers and onion into wide strips.
  2. Toss vegetables on a sheet pan with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano. Roast 15 min.
  3. Nestle the sausages (whole or cut into coins) among the vegetables. Roast another 20–25 min, tossing once, until sausage is 160°F and potatoes are browned.
  4. Finish under the broiler 2 min for extra color if you like, then serve straight from the pan.

Publix Sweet Italian Sausage is dairy/egg-free, but glance at the label if you swap brands — some fresh sausages add milk-based fillers.

Garlic-Lime Shrimp & Yellow Rice Bowls

30 minserves 4 seafoodquickweeknight

Quick-seared white shrimp over saffron-y yellow rice, loaded with charred peppers, corn, and cool avocado.

On sale this week

  • Publix Mahatma Yellow Rice Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Tricolor Peppers $4.99
  • Publix Bulk Sweet Corn 5 for $3.00
  • Publix Green Avocados 2 for $5.00

From the pantry

  • garlic
  • lime
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • Cholula or hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the yellow rice per the package. Cut corn kernels off the cob; dice peppers.
  2. Sear peppers and corn in a hot skillet with a little oil until charred at the edges, 4–5 min; set aside.
  3. Pat shrimp dry, toss with garlic, salt, and a squeeze of lime. Sear 1–2 min per side until just pink.
  4. Build bowls: rice, charred veg, shrimp, sliced avocado, more lime, and a shake of Cholula.

Use plain White Shrimp here, not the pre-breaded panko shrimp — breaded coatings often contain egg.

Chicken & Broccoli Stir-Fry over Jasmine Rice

30 minserves 4 quickone-pan proteinweeknight

A fast, savory stir-fry of sliced chicken breast and broccoli in a glossy garlic-soy sauce over fluffy jasmine rice.

On sale this week

  • Publix GreenWise Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast $4.99 lb
  • Publix Broccoli or Cauliflower 2 for $6.00
  • Publix Mahatma Jasmine Rice $2.00
  • Publix Publix Yellow Onions $3.49
  • Kroger Springer Mountain Farms Boneless Chicken Breasts Buy 1 Get 1 FREE (swap option)

From the pantry

  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • fresh ginger (or ground)
  • cornstarch
  • neutral oil
  • brown sugar or honey

Steps

  1. Start the jasmine rice: rinse, simmer covered with a pinch of salt ~15 min, rest off heat.
  2. Slice chicken thin; toss with 1 tsp cornstarch and a splash of soy. Cut broccoli into florets, onion into wedges.
  3. Stir-fry chicken in hot oil until golden and cooked through; remove. Stir-fry broccoli and onion 3–4 min with a splash of water to steam.
  4. Return chicken, add a sauce of 3 tbsp soy, 1 tsp sugar, garlic, ginger, and 1 tsp cornstarch in 1/4 cup water. Toss until glossy; serve over rice.

Naturally dairy/egg-free. Kroger has Springer Mountain chicken breast BOGO this week too if Publix is picked over.

Ground Beef & Mushroom Bolognese

40 minserves 4 pastaweeknight

A rich, meaty red sauce with baby bella mushrooms and onion, simmered into a jar of Rao's and tossed with pasta.

On sale this week

  • Publix Steakhouse Elite Signature Ground Beef $9.99
  • Publix Publix Baby Bella Whole Mushrooms 2 for $4.00
  • Publix Rao's Homemade Sauce $6.99
  • Publix Ronzoni Pasta Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Yellow Onions $3.49

From the pantry

  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • salt & pepper
  • dried oregano

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and cook the Ronzoni pasta to al dente; reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a little olive oil, breaking it up; season with salt and pepper. Remove excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onion and sliced mushrooms; cook 6–8 min until soft and browned. Stir in garlic and oregano.
  4. Pour in the Rao's sauce, simmer 10 min, loosen with pasta water, then toss with the pasta and serve.

Rao's marinara is dairy/egg-free — skip the parmesan for a dairy-free bowl, or top with a dairy-free grated cheese. Barilla pasta is $1.39 at Kroger this week if you'd rather stock up there.

Salsa-Simmered Pork Tacos with Avocado

35 minserves 4 taco nightone-pan proteinweeknight

Marinated pork tenderloin seared and simmered in salsa until saucy, piled into warm tortillas with avocado and peppers.

On sale this week

  • Publix Smithfield Marinated Pork Tenderloin Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Salsa Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Green Avocados 2 for $5.00
  • Publix Publix Tricolor Peppers $4.99
  • Kroger Pork Boston Butt Buy 1 Get 1 FREE (budget swap)

From the pantry

  • corn or flour tortillas
  • lime
  • salt
  • cumin
  • cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut pork tenderloin into strips or bite-size pieces; season with a little cumin and salt.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet until browned, 4–5 min. Add sliced peppers and cook 3 min.
  3. Pour in the salsa, lower heat, and simmer 8–10 min until the pork is cooked through and the sauce clings.
  4. Warm tortillas, fill with pork, and top with sliced avocado, a squeeze of lime, and cilantro.

Skip the sour cream and cheese to keep it dairy-free — the avocado does the creamy work. For a slow, cheap crowd version, use Kroger's BOGO Boston Butt and simmer longer.
