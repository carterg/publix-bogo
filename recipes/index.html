BBQ Chicken with Charred Corn & Crispy Potatoes

45 minserves 4 grillfamily favorite

Grilled (or seared) chicken glazed with BBQ sauce, with charred sweet corn and smashed roasted potatoes.

On sale this week

  • Kroger Perdue Boneless Chicken Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Kroger Sweet Corn & Baker Russet Potatoes $2.99 / 4 ct
  • Publix Sweet Baby Ray's Barbecue Sauce Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Fire & Smoke Seasoning Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Pompeian Imported Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • salt & pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes whole until just fork-tender, ~15 min. Drain, smash lightly, toss with olive oil and seasoning, and roast at 450°F until crispy, ~20 min.
  2. Season chicken with Fire & Smoke rub. Grill or sear over medium-high heat until cooked through (165°F), brushing with BBQ sauce in the last few minutes.
  3. Char the corn alongside the chicken, turning until spotted all over. Rest the chicken 5 min, glaze once more, and serve.

Sweet Baby Ray's original is dairy/egg-free; skip any butter on the corn — olive oil + seasoning works great.

Shrimp Tacos with Avocado-Lime Crema

25 minserves 4 quickseafood

Crispy or sautéed shrimp in crunchy taco shells with a dairy-free avocado crema standing in for sour cream.

On sale this week

  • Publix Publix Medium Shrimp Platter (or Gorton's Shrimp) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Old El Paso Taco Shells or Tortillas Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Publix Red Seedless Watermelon Chunks Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • avocado
  • lime
  • cilantro
  • shredded cabbage or lettuce
  • chili powder
  • cumin

Steps

  1. Blend one avocado with the juice of a lime, a handful of cilantro, and a pinch of salt until smooth — that's the crema.
  2. Toss shrimp with chili powder and cumin, then sauté 2–3 min per side (or bake breaded shrimp per the box).
  3. Warm the shells, fill with cabbage, shrimp, and crema. Serve with watermelon on the side.

Plain shrimp is safe; if you go with Gorton's breaded shrimp, check the label — some breaded varieties contain dairy.

Smoky Kielbasa & Tomato Rice Skillet

35 minserves 4 one-pan

One-pan browned kielbasa simmered with tomatoes, peppers, and rice.

On sale this week

  • Publix Hillshire Farm Smoked Sausage or Polska Kielbasa Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix SMT Tomatoes (28-oz can) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Pompeian Imported Olive Oil Buy 1 Get 1 FREE

From the pantry

  • long-grain rice
  • onion
  • bell pepper
  • garlic
  • smoked paprika
  • chicken broth or water

Steps

  1. Slice kielbasa into coins and brown in olive oil; set aside.
  2. Sauté onion, pepper, and garlic in the same pan. Stir in 1 cup rice and a teaspoon of smoked paprika.
  3. Add the can of tomatoes plus 1½ cups broth, return the kielbasa, cover, and simmer ~20 min until the rice is tender.

Stick with the original smoked sausage — skip the cheddar variety.

Classic grilled burgers with grillin' beans and corn — condiments are on sale too.

From the pantry

Steps

  1. Form ¼-lb patties, season generously, and press a dimple into the center of each.
  2. Grill 3–4 min per side. Warm the beans in a pot on the side of the grill and char the corn.
  3. Toast the buns, build with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles — skip cheese and mayo.

Nature's Own classic buns are typically dairy/egg-free, but check the label on the variety you grab. Veggie swap: Actual Veggies Black Bean Burgers are also BOGO at Publix this week.

Sticky BBQ Ribs with Corn & Watermelon

2.5–3 hrs (mostly hands-off)serves 4 weekendhands-off

Low-effort oven ribs (they come pre-seasoned in the bag) finished with a sticky glaze.

On sale this week

  • Publix Publix St. Louis Style Pork Spareribs (frozen, in-bag) Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Publix Kosmos Q Rub or BBQ Sauce Buy 1 Get 1 FREE
  • Kroger Kroger Sweet Corn $2.99 / 4 ct
  • Kroger Whole Seedless Watermelon $3.99 (save $2)

From the pantry

  • foil

Steps

  1. Thaw ribs overnight in the fridge. Add extra rub if you like, wrap tightly in foil, and bake at 300°F for ~2½ hours until tender.
  2. Unwrap, brush with BBQ sauce, and broil (or grill) 5 min until sticky and caramelized.
  3. Serve with charred corn and cold watermelon.

The Prairie Fresh pre-seasoned ribs (also BOGO) work identically — glance at the marinade ingredients to confirm no dairy.
